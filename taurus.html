There's a good chance they can cook like nobody's business, but even if they can't, they know how to make mac and cheese like a boss.

Negatives

Here's the problem with Tauruses...

The concept of 'Multitasking' doesn't apply to them.Things can turn ugly when you find yourself in an arguement with a Taurian.
